WHAT WE DO

  1. EDUCATE

    Navigating the new blockchain space is scary for new students, and we are here to help. With a variety of workshops covering both technical and non-technical concepts, we will provide an insightful acumen that will gear you for the technology of tomorrow. We believe everyone should have fundamental knowledge on a technology as disruptive as the internet.

  2. CONNECT

    We host panels, lectures & workshops, connecting Australia's most aspiring blockchain talent with industry leaders, experts and other supporters.

  3. UPSKILL

    We believe blockchain technology takes not only coders, but lawyers, economists, entrepreneurs, and anyone with a passion for disruptive tech. We will cover a variety of topics, from NFTs, to Defi protocols & scalability solutions like Layer 2s.
